receive your mobile device location in order to show you the offers and deals from businesses near your location.

Usage Information

For our internal purposes and to continuously improve the user experience, we collect information about the basic use of the application such as clicks, favorite stores and favorite offers. This personal information is only used by HawaiiQpons to provide a better user experience and is not supplied to anyone outside HawaiiQpons. Email

Sharing an email address or logging in with a Facebook profile is not required to enjoy the many benefits of the HawaiiQpons mobile application. If you do share your email with HawaiiQpons directly or through a partner promotional offer, we will protect your contact information and never sell it to third parties. We use your email address to inform you about the new features and new versions of HawaiiQpons mobile app. You may occasionally receive email from us about new coupons or sales from your favorite retailers as well as preview of some special offers and other noteworthy items related to shopping. We hope you will find the information about the updates and offers within the email interesting, relevant and useful. Of course, if you would rather not receive this information, you may opt-out of receiving such emails by following the instructions at the bottom of each email or by contacting us at support@thisweek.com.
